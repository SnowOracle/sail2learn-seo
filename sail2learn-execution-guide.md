# Sail2Learn SEO Execution Guide: Step-by-Step Instructions

This guide provides the exact click-by-click instructions for executing the high-impact tasks outlined in our SEO strategy. Follow these steps to implement the changes.

---

## TIER 1: CRITICAL IMPACT (Execute First)

### Task 1: Create the Google Business Profile
*This is the single most important task for local SEO dominance.*

**Step-by-Step:**
1. **Sign In:** Go to [google.com/business](https://google.com/business) and sign in with the Google account you want associated with the business.
2. **Add Business:** Click "Manage now" or "Add your business to Google".
3. **Business Name:** Enter "Sail2Learn" (or "Sail to Learn").
4. **Category:** Choose "Sailing School" as the primary business category.
5. **Location Setup (Crucial):**
   * Google will ask: *“Do you want to add a location customers can visit, like a store or office?”* 
   * **Select "No"** (since you meet at a public boat ramp, not a dedicated office).
   * **Service Area:** Add "Sarasota, FL" and "Bradenton, FL" as your service areas.
6. **Contact Info:** Enter the official public phone number and set the website URL to `https://sail2learn.com`.
7. **Verification:** Google will ask you to verify the business (usually via a postcard to a home address or a video verification). Follow their steps.
8. **Optimization (Once Verified):**
   * Upload photos of the boat, Steve, and students.
   * Generate your "Review Link" from the Google Business dashboard.
   * Email past students who left website testimonials and ask them to paste their reviews onto Google using that link.

### Task 2: Fix the Homepage H1 & Meta Description (in Squarespace)
*This tells Google exactly what the website is about so it ranks for the right keywords.*

**Step-by-Step for the H1 Tag:**
1. Log into Squarespace and go to **Pages**.
2. Click on your **Homepage** to open the preview, then click **Edit** in the top left.
3. Locate the very first text block at the top of the page (where it currently says "sail to learn").
4. Highlight that text. A formatting toolbar will appear above it.
5. Change the format dropdown from `Paragraph` (or whatever it currently is) to **`Heading 1`**.
6. Change the text to read: **"Sarasota Sailing Lessons on Sarasota Bay"** (or something similar containing your primary keywords).
7. Click **Save** in the top left corner.

**Step-by-Step for the Meta Description:**
1. In the main Squarespace menu, go to **Settings** > **SEO Appearance**.
2. Scroll down to the **SEO Site Description** box.
3. Paste in this exact text:
   *Learn to sail in Sarasota Bay with Sail2Learn. Beginner to intermediate hands-on sailing lessons in Sarasota and Bradenton, FL led by an ASA-certified instructor.*
4. Click **Save**.

### Task 3: Image Compression & Alt Text
*This fixes performance penalties and helps visually impaired users and AI bots understand your photos.*

**Step-by-Step for Compression:**
1. **Download:** Download all the large, raw photos you intend to use on the site to your computer.
2. **Compress:** Go to [TinyPNG.com](https://tinypng.com/) (or a similar free tool) and upload the images.
3. **Download & Rename:** Download the compressed versions. *Crucial:* Rename the files on your computer to include keywords (e.g., change `IMG_3103.jpg` to `sarasota-sailing-lesson.jpg`).
4. **Replace in Squarespace:** Delete the old heavy images from your Squarespace gallery/pages and upload the new, lightweight, renamed ones.

**Step-by-Step for Alt Text:**
1. In Squarespace Edit mode, click on an image.
2. Click the **Pencil Icon (Edit)**.
3. Look for the **Alt Text** field (usually under the Content or Design tab).
4. Write a descriptive sentence: *"Student steering a 19' Flying Scot sailboat during a beginner lesson on Sarasota Bay."* (Note: You can leave the owner's visible "meditated captions" exactly as they are—Alt text is hidden!).

## TIER 2: MODERATE IMPACT (Execute Second)

### Task 4: Build the "Entity" Profile for AIO (AI Optimization)
*This proves to ChatGPT and Google that Steve is a verified expert.*

**Step-by-Step:**
1. Go to the page where Steve's bio lives (or the Homepage). Click **Edit**.
2. Find the text mentioning his certifications: *"Bonham is a certified ASA (American Sailing Association) instructor..."*
3. Highlight "ASA (American Sailing Association)" and click the **Link icon** in the toolbar.
4. Paste the URL to the official ASA website (`https://asa.com/`). Turn on "Open in New Window".
5. Do the same for "US Sailing" if mentioned.
6. Make sure his name is written clearly as "Captain Steve Bonham" or "Instructor Steve Bonham" near his credentials.

---

### Task 5: Inject Local Business Schema Markup
*This feeds exact data directly to Googlebot and AI crawlers.*

**Step-by-Step:**
1. Once you have the official public phone number, email, and price range, have your AI assistant (me!) generate the JSON-LD Schema code block.
2. Copy that code block.
3. In Squarespace, go to **Website** > **Pages** > scroll down to **Website Tools** > **Code Injection** (or **Settings > Advanced > Code Injection** depending on your Squarespace version).
4. Paste the code into the **HEADER** box.
5. Click **Save**.
