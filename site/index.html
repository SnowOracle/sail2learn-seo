Learn with us

Sail2Learn provides beginner to intermediate sailing lessons throughout Sarasota and Bradenton, FL. Our approach is simple: learn by doing. Every lesson is hands-on, active, and designed to make you a confident, capable sailor.

As a small, local business, Sail to Learn focuses on personal instruction and close guidance. We ensure that every student receives individual attention while learning essential sailing skills on the water.

Whether you’re completely new to sailing or looking to strengthen your fundamentals, Sail2Learn offers a supportive and engaging way to experience the beauty and excitement of sailing in Sarasota Bay.

Meet your teacher

Steve Bonham — a calm, patient, experienced (50+ years sailing) and friendly skipper.

Bonham is a certified ASA (American Sailing Association) instructor for ASA 101 — Keelboat Sailing, ASA 203 — Coastal Cruising, and ASA 204 — Bareboat Cruising. Bonham is also a US Sailing certified Windsurfing Instructor.

Bonham is a former college health & kinesiology professor, an NCAA Division I men’s gymnastics coach, and an instructional systems designer / faculty developer. He believes that for learning to stick it must be hands-on and immediately relevant.

Authority & trust (SEO / AIO): Learn more about credentials at American Sailing Association and US Sailing.
