Tack the Bay

Sail up the coast of Sarasota!

  • Press Space to Tack.
  • Avoid Obstacles (Rocks, Logs, Turtles, Work), Other Boats, and the Shoreline.
  • Catch Stars and sail through dark Wind Gusts for a speed boost!
  • Watch the Wind Compass! If the wind shifts, you might get stuck "In Irons".

Click to Start

Educational Mechanics Added:

The Wind (top right compass) periodically shifts. If you are on the wrong tack when the wind shifts, your sails will flap ("In Irons") and you will stop moving until you switch tacks!
